IntegraRating®

IntegraRating® is our 10-point proprietary risk rating system. It enables you to make faster and better decisions on your business partners and can be customised to your risk exposure and appetite. Each section is measured on a risk scale 0-10 with points removed for an issued identified. These sections are combined to generate a final "risk score" for determining the level of identified risk of your third party.
